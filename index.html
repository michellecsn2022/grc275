BroKen Designs

Hello! My name is Michelle, and my main hobbies are converting my graphic design work into gifts and apparel for my friends and family. I call this hobby the "Bro-Ken Designs" venture, as it is a combination of my kids' names - Brody and Mackenzie.

I like digitally designing and then creating branded monograms. Then I would either heat transfer or embroider them on apparel as gifts. For example, I've created hangers and t-shirts for my son's hockey teams and I've designed a wedding monogram for a friend for guest giveaway.

In addition, I also like creating fun videos, but these take a lot of time to make. Here is an example of one that I made for my son's 2020-2021 house league hockey season: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=xpjNbJ5TRdg
